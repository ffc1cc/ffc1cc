Introduction to Management

Instructor: Eunyoung Cheon

Contact: echeon@yonsei.ac.kr

Credits: 3

Course Materials: Gareth Jones and Jennifer George. Essentials of Contemporary Management, McGraw Hill.
*Additional reading materials will be announced in the class.

This course is a study of management concepts, emphasizing the management functions of planning, decision-making, organizing, leading and controlling. The purpose of this course is to introduce students to a comprehensive introduction to effective management principles and conduct. This course is designed to not only provide students with an introduction to contemporary management concepts and skills but also encourage students to put these concepts and skills into practice. It prepares students with the most contemporary and up-to-date account of the changing environment of management and management practice. By the end of the class, students will be equipped with management competence for their future career.
